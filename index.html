Display System

The theatre features an LG LAPA163 All-in-One LED display — a 163-inch direct-view LED wall delivering a seamless, bezel-free 4K UHD canvas. Pixel pitch of approximately 1.58 mm ensures sharp text and vivid imagery from the nearest executive seating row at 3.4 metres. The all-in-one form factor integrates the LED cabinet, controller, and power supply into a single package, eliminating external video processors.

Content sources include the Crestron AirMedia wireless presentation system for BYOD connectivity, the digital podium's integrated PC, and any HDMI/USB-C connected devices routed through the Crestron switcher.

Audio System

Two A-Array Vyper-102 compact line array modules are mounted on either side of the screen, providing uniform SPL coverage across the tiered seating from Level +100 mm to Level +400 mm. The tight vertical pattern control minimises ceiling and rear-wall reflections.

Low-frequency reinforcement comes from dual KU210 subwoofers at floor level, delivering extended bass response down to 40 Hz for cinematic content. The K104 four-channel amplifier drives the system with DSP processing for crossover, EQ, delay alignment, and limiter protection.

A Sennheiser EW-D ME3 wireless headset microphone provides broadcast-quality speech pickup with freedom of movement around the podium area.

Control Architecture

The Crestron CP4-R processor orchestrates all AV sources, display power, audio levels, lighting scenes, and presentation switching from a unified platform. The TS-1080-B touch panel at the podium provides one-touch scene recall. The AirMedia AM3-212-I gateway enables seamless BYOD content sharing to the 163″ display without cables.

Lighting Control — Lutron HomeWorks QS

All theatre lighting operates on the Lutron HomeWorks QS platform with DALI digital dimming. The QS processor communicates with the DALI module to control each luminaire individually or in grouped zones — from full wash for cleaning, to "Presenter + Screen" with aisle markers only, to full "Cinema" blackout.

Two Pico wireless keypads (entry + podium) provide quick scene recall. Two ceiling occupancy sensors enable auto-on/auto-off (default 20-minute vacancy timeout) for energy efficiency.

Design Language

The interior follows a warm, premium corporate aesthetic — natural wood veneer and fluted timber panelling against dark theatre walls, with integrated greenery in foyer and transition zones. The palette: oak-tone veneer, dark charcoal carpet, light timber ceiling baffles with recessed lighting, and curated lounge furniture in the breakout space.

Floating Ceiling & Lighting

The suspended timber-baffle ceiling is the theatre's signature element — linear baffles with integrated LED profiles between louvers creating rhythmic light and shadow patterns. Deep recessed profiles (30W/m) between baffles provide ambient wash, while profile lights (25W/m) mark cove edges and step risers. All fixtures are DALI-2 addressable for scene control via the Lutron HomeWorks QS system.
